# Optimize Local Connect Roadmap

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

Current Version: MVP Phase 1 Marketplace

Optimize Local Connect is building an operating system connecting Property Managers and Local Service Vendors. The goal is to simplify vendor discovery, service requests, communication, and long-term business relationships. This roadmap should always reflect current production status.
